ABOUT

Our Heritage,
Global Agility

With over 25 years of deep industry expertise, Intertrans was founded by maritime professionals dedicated to redefining global logistics. What began as a focused maritime operation has steadily evolved into a comprehensive maritime and commodities partner.

Today, we proudly operate a versatile fleet of owned and long-term chartered vessels while continuing to offer top-tier ship brokerage services.

Driven by our commitment to growth and client success, we have successfully expanded our footprint into global grain trading and specialized agricultural brokerage. This strategic expansion allows us to seamlessly cover the entire value chain, bridging the gap between producers and global markets.

At the very core of Intertrans is a steadfast belief in partnership. We recognize that true success is built on a foundation of reliability, transparency, and trust. By fostering enduring relationships with our clients and global partners, we ensure that every cargo is handled with the utmost care and professional integrity.
